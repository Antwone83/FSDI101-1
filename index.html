Bio of Antwone D. Adams

Antwone D. Adams is a native of Washington, DC by the way of Oxon Hill, Maryland and graduate of Friendly Senior High School, Fort Washington, Maryland. Mindful of the privilege to serve his fellow Americans, he enlisted in the United States Navy on August 29, 2001. Upon completion of Recruit Training, Great Lakes, Illinios and Operation Specialist "A" school in Dam Neck, VA, he was assigned to USS Duluth (LPD 6) in San Diego, CA.

His follow-on tours included USS Nimitz (CVN 68), NRD Richmond Virginia, COMUSNAVCENT Fifth Fleet, COMLOGFORNAVCENT, USS Benfold (DDG 65), Training Support Center SD and USS Spruance (DDG 111). During his time serving in the Navy, Antwone became a published photographer and Disc Jockey. After 20 years in the military, he retired August 31, 2021 and resides in San Diego.
